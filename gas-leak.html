Gas Leak | Date Unknown

As I went to the basement to grab some food from the freezer I noticed a very recognizable smell - gas. I immediately got a spray bottle with soap and water and sprayed our gas lines where the smell was coming from. Bubbles rapidly developed and the leak was located quickly.

For an emergency like this I circumvented reaching out to M/I Homes corporate warranty and repair departmeent and reached out direclty to the construction manager for the neighborhood. We were very fortunate in that a plumber was at another house that was being built and they were able to come out and repair this gas leak within an hour.

I cannot help but imagine a scenario where this could have occurred and I didn't go to the basement to get food from the freezer, or if we were out of town, or any other number of situations where this would not have been caught and the potential outcome from those scenarios.

As you'll notice there are not any pictures of this situation as that was the last thing on everyones mind as this situation was being handled.
